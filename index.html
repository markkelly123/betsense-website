Would you use an app that tracks how much money you spend on gambling so you can stay in control?

77% of those we asked said they would.

You're a recreational gambler. You wouldn't even describe yourself as a 'gambler' per se...a 'punter' maybe. You play the lotto, the occasional scratchie, a flutter on the horses come spring, a couple of multi's on the footy with mates, you compete in a fantasy football league, and you don't mind the odd punt on the pokies. All normal Australian things that everyone does.

But Australia has a problem with gambling harm. The societal costs of gambling – including adverse financial impacts, emotional and psychological costs, relationship and family impacts, and productivity loss and work impacts – have been estimated at around $7 billion in Victoria alone. The purpose of BetSense is to ensure you don't ever become part of those statistics.

We're building BetSense - a safer gambling app that will consider your income and bills and compare it to how much you spend across all types of gambling. It will then let you know if your total spend is healthy or whether you should be more cautious. It's a simple idea to help you stay in control, bet sensibly, and prevent gambling harm.

The app will be independent of any gambling operator or government agency, and your information will be private and completely confidential...a tool for your eyes and yours alone.
